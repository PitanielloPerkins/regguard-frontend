## SECTION 3: INTERCONNECTION PROCESS (LARGE-LOAD PATH)

### ERCOT Large-Load Procedure (Nodal Market)

**Phase 1: Feasibility Study (12–18 months)**
- Oncor determines if system can support 250 MW at your POI
- Cost to applicant: typically $10K–$50K (shared study costs)
- Deliverable: Feasibility study with preliminary upgrade needs and cost estimate

**Phase 2: System Impact Study (12–18 months if Phase 1 says "feasible")**
- Detailed network upgrade analysis; protective relay coordination
- Cost: typically $50K–$200K
- Deliverable: Final network upgrade costs and interconnection diagram

**Phase 3: Facilities Study (6–12 months if Phase 2 approved)**
- Design final substation/equipment layout
- Cost: typically $50K–$150K
- Deliverable: Interconnection agreement, final facility design, start date for connection

### Total Timeline: 36–54+ months (3–4.5 years) from Phase 1 start to energization
