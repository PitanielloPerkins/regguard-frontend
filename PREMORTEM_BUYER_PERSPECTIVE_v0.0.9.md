## FRICTION POINTS (Why I might not buy)

### Friction #1: No Proof of Concept
**My question:** "Can you show me an actual report from a real site?"

**Current answer:** "See the sample report template in the documentation"

**My reaction:** "That's... a template. Not a real example. Templates are always polished. What about edge cases? What about accuracy?"

**Would fix my concern?**
- ✓ Show 1–2 anonymized real reports (redact company names, addresses)
- ✓ Show before/after: "Here's what the customer found useful"
- ✓ Show one report that identifies a bad site (moratorium, zoning conflict)

### Friction #2: No Free Trial
**My question:** "Can I try it on one of my actual sites before committing $15K?"

**Current answer:** Nothing on the site addresses this

**My reaction:** "No trial, no demo, no free run? That's risky. I'm not paying $15K blind."

**Would fix my concern?**
- ✓ Free trial: "Try RegGuard on 1 site free, see the results. If you like it, pay $15K to download the PDF package."
- ✓ Freemium: "Get the research memo free (text format), pay $15K to unlock punch list + permit package PDFs"
- ✓ Money-back guarantee: "Pay now, try it. If you hate it, full refund within 7 days."

### Friction #3: No Social Proof
**My question:** "Has anyone else used this? Did it work for them?"

**Current answer:** Nothing on the site

**My reaction:** "No testimonials, no case studies, no reviews. For all I know, this is brand new and untested."

**Would fix my concern?**
- ✓ Testimonials: "This saved us 2 weeks of research" (actual customers)
- ✓ Case study: "Data center in Texas, $500K project, RegGuard found moratorium, saved $100K"
- ✓ Numbers: "500+ reports generated, 95% accuracy rating"

### Friction #4: No Risk Mitigation
**My question:** "What if the report is wrong and I make a bad decision?"

**Current answer:** Legal disclaimer says "get your attorney to review" (hides behind counsel)

**My reaction:** "That doesn't help me. If I'm paying $15K for research, I need confidence it's right."

**Would fix my concern?**
- ✓ "Accuracy guarantee: We stand behind our research. If a critical finding is wrong, we refund the full $15K."
- ✓ "We cite all sources—you can verify independently before acting."
- ✓ "70+ hours of combined RTO/permitting expertise behind each report"

### Friction #5: Unclear ROI for My Specific Situation
**My question:** "Will this actually save me money on MY projects?"

**Current answer:** "Kill one bad site = saves $100K" (generic example)

**My reaction:** "That's a nice headline, but my projects might not fit that scenario. What if all my sites are good? What if the ROI is less obvious?"

**Would fix my concern?**
- ✓ Interactive ROI calculator: "Enter your typical project cost, timeline. See how much RegGuard saves."
- ✓ Scenario-based pricing: "Screening 1 site? $15K. Screening 5 sites? $12K each."
- ✓ "Most customers break even on 2 bad sites discovered = save $200K+, only spend $30K on reports"
