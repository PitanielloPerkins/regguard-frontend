# RegGuard Enterprise Sales Guide

## Target Segments & Sales Strategy

This guide is for the **20% of potential buyers** who are good fits for the **$60K/year Enterprise plan** rather than the Hybrid ($15K + $20K/yr) model.

---

## SEGMENT 1: Infrastructure PE Firms (HIGHEST VALUE)

### Who They Are
- Portfolio companies managing data center, renewable, or grid infrastructure investments
- Typical: DigitalBridge, Blackstone Infrastructure, Apollo InfraBuild, Stonepeak, IPI Partners
- Deal flow: 10+ interconnection projects/year across portfolio

### Why They Buy Enterprise
- **Diligence for 10+ portfolio companies** across multiple states/RTOs
- Need consistent methodology and turnaround across team
- Use RegGuard output for investment committee memos and lender briefings
- Often white-label or rebrand outputs for LPs (limited partners)

### Pain Points RegGuard Solves
- Portfolio firms waste $500K–$2M/year on duplicate interconnection diligence (each company independently hires counsel)
- RegGuard creates **single research source** → cost per site drops from $75K to $6K/yr (at Enterprise volume)
- Standardized reports enable cross-portfolio benchmarking

### Sales Strategy (No Cold Calls)
1. **Target:** Development VP or Chief Development Officer at PE firm
2. **Entry point:** Partner with their existing IC consultant (see Partner Channel section below) who refers RegGuard
3. **Proof point:** Pilot RegGuard on 1–2 portfolio companies' sites → demonstrate time/cost savings → upgrade to annual contract
4. **Land & expand:** Once contracted, offer quarterly strategic review calls + custom integrations

### Typical Sales Cycle
- **Time to pilot:** 3–4 weeks (one report)
- **Time to contract:** 6–8 weeks (if pilot successful)
- **Deal size:** $60K/year

### LTV Potential
- **Year 1:** $60K
- **Year 2–3:** $60K + expanded scope (added portfolio companies) = $100K–$200K
- **3-year LTV:** $200K–$400K

## SEGMENT 2: Large Interconnection Consulting Firms (HIGH VALUE)

### Who They Are
- 10–50 person boutiques specializing in grid interconnection (EPC/IC path)
- Examples: niche energy consulting, ex-utility IC teams, specialized EPC practices
- Deal flow: 15–40 IC studies/year for clients

### Why They Buy Enterprise
- **White-label / rebrand** RegGuard research → resell to clients at 2–3× markup
- Convert client relationship: "We use RegGuard for preliminary research → helps us quote Phase 1 scopes better"
- Faster turnaround = higher project margins (less staff time)

### Pain Points RegGuard Solves
- Consultants spend **2–3 weeks per site** on AHJ/RTO research before engaging studies → high billable overhead
- RegGuard cuts that to **2 days** → consultants can bid Phase 1 scopes faster and more accurately
- Margin improvement: if consultant charges $50K to develop Phase 1 scope, cutting research time by 80% = $10K margin gain per client project

### Sales Strategy
1. **Target:** Managing Partner or VP Business Development
2. **Positioning:** "Accelerate your intake research → higher margins on Phase 1 scopes, happier clients"
3. **Offer:** 20% partner revenue share on RegGuard reports + $60K annual Enterprise license for unlimited internal use
4. **Proof:** Demo on their next 2–3 projects; measure turnaround improvement
5. **Contractual:** Partner agreement specifies white-label terms (they can use your branding or theirs)

### Typical Sales Cycle
- **Time to pilot:** 2–3 weeks (one joint report with their client)
- **Time to partner agreement:** 4–8 weeks (legal terms around white-label, revenue share)
- **Revenue model:** 15–20% rev share on reports they resell + $60K annual license

### LTV Potential
- **Direct (annual license):** $60K/year
- **Partner channel (rev share):** If consultant generates 10 reports/year at $20K markup, RegGuard gets $40K (at 20% rev share) = $100K/year total
- **3-year LTV:** $360K–$480K

## SEGMENT 3: Large Data Center Developers (MEDIUM-HIGH VALUE)

### Who They Are
- Developers with permanent site acquisition teams (10+ MW/year of projects)
- Examples: CyrusOne-scale regional operators, hyperscaler-adjacent groups, large wholesale DC developers
- Deal flow: 5+ site screens/year

### Why They Buy Enterprise
- **Continuous pipeline:** Always screening sites; diligence is recurring operating cost
- Budget justification: $60K/year is cheaper than hiring 1 FTE analyst (loaded: $120K+)
- Volume discount makes sense: if they'd do 5 reports, regguard pays for itself vs. per-project pricing

### Pain Points RegGuard Solves
- Large devs don't want to call counsel every time they screen a new site (slow, expensive, relationship-dependent)
- RegGuard becomes **in-house research tool** for site viability screening
- Reduces decision cycle: "Should we pursue this site?" → RegGuard report in 2 days instead of waiting 2 weeks for counsel

### Sales Strategy
1. **Target:** SVP of Development or Director of Acquisitions
2. **Positioning:** "Reduce your IC due diligence cycle. Evaluate sites in days instead of weeks."
3. **Offer:** $60K/year Enterprise; 5–10 reports included (typical for their volume)
4. **Proof:** Pilot on their next 2–3 sites; measure actual cost savings vs. their counsel spend
5. **Expansion:** After 6 months, upsell to custom integrations (e.g., API feed into their internal deal tracking)

### Typical Sales Cycle
- **Time to pilot:** 2–3 weeks (1–2 reports)
- **Time to contract:** 6–12 weeks (annual commitment requires CFO/procurement buy-in)

### LTV Potential
- **Year 1:** $60K
- **Year 2–3:** $60K + potential expansion to other regions / portfolio companies
- **3-year LTV:** $180K–$300K

## SEGMENT 4: Hyperscale/Metacloud Site Teams (LOWER VALUE, BUT LONG-TERM)

### Who They Are
- Google, Meta, Microsoft, Amazon internal site acquisition teams
- Hyperscalers have dedicated RE teams with large annual diligence budgets
- Deal flow: 10–30+ sites/year

### Why They Buy Enterprise
- **Scale:** Hyperscalers screen 20–100 sites to build 3–5 data centers
- **Cost irrelevance:** $60K/year is immaterial to a team with $1M+ annual diligence budgets
- **Speed:** Hyperscalers need fast preliminary screening across multiple geographies simultaneously

### Pain Points RegGuard Solves
- Large diligence consulting firms (Black & Veatch, WSP) can't keep up with their pace
- RegGuard provides **independent preliminary research** before engaging EPC studies
- Reduces consultant dependency on preliminary work

### Sales Strategy
1. **Target:** Real Estate/Infrastructure Director
2. **Entry:** Almost impossible without warm intro. Requires partnership with consulting firm they already use, or competitor relationships.
3. **Positioning:** "Preliminary RTO research automation for your site screening pipeline"
4. **Deal structure:** Usually bundled with larger consulting/implementation deal (not standalone)

### Typical Sales Cycle
- **Time to pilot:** 8–12 weeks (org-wide review, security/legal)
- **Time to contract:** 3–6 months (procurement process, RFP, IT security review)

### LTV Potential
- **Year 1:** $60K (but can expand to $200K–$500K if bundled with consulting)
- **3-year LTV:** $300K–$1.5M (if they scale across multiple regions)

## PARTNER CHANNEL (CRITICAL FOR ENTERPRISE GROWTH)

### IC Consultant Partners (Best Channel)

**Target:** 3–5 small IC consulting firms (10–50 person boutiques)

**Offer:** 
- 20% revenue share on every RegGuard report they resell to clients
- $60K annual Enterprise license (unlimited internal use + white-label rights)
- Co-marketing (they mention RegGuard in sales decks; you mention them as preferred IC partner)

**Why this works:**
- IC consultants are the *gatekeepers* to data center developers
- RegGuard becomes their **intake research tool** → faster, higher-margin Phase 1 scopes
- Consultants resell at 2–3× markup ($15K RegGuard → $40K–$50K client-facing cost) → they make margin on RegGuard; RegGuard gets rev share

**Expected volume:**
- Each partner consultant generates 15–30 RegGuard reports/year (resold to clients)
- At 20% rev share = $45K–$90K/year per partner
- 5 partners = $225K–$450K annual rev from channel

**Partner acquisition:**
1. Identify 5 top IC consultants (via LinkedIn, industry directories, LinkedIn group recommendations)
2. Email: "RegGuard accelerates intake research. Resell to your clients at markup; you keep 80%, we keep 20%."
3. Demo on their next project
4. Partner agreement in 4–6 weeks

## SALES MESSAGING (ANCHOR TO LAW FIRMS, NOT SaaS)

### DO
- **"60–80% cheaper than law firm diligence"** (anchor to $75K–$150K counsel, not $29/mo software)
- **"48 hours vs. 6 weeks"** (speed is the anchor, not features)
- **"Kill bad sites before spending $100K on studies"** (ROI, not product)
- **"Accelerates intake; your counsel reviews before filing"** (we don't replace counsel; we enable them)

### DON'T
- "AI compliance platform" (too generic; sounds like Procore)
- "Unlimited reports for $60K/year" (sounds cheap; devalues the product)
- "Advanced NLP + web research" (buyers don't care about tech; they care about speed/cost)
- "Replaces interconnection consultants" (creates friction with consultant channel)

## KEY DIFFERENTIATOR vs. Law Firms

**Law firms:**
- $75K–$150K per site
- 2–4 week turnaround
- Legal opinion; defensible in litigation
- Relationship-dependent; hard to scale

**RegGuard:**
- $15K per site (hybrid) / $6K per site (at Enterprise volume)
- 48 hours
- Research memo + worksheets; attorney reviews before filing
- Scalable, repeatable, fast

**Where RegGuard wins:** Speed + cost on **preliminary intake** (not final legal/engineering).  
**Where law firms win:** Legal defensibility + final due diligence (RegGuard is upstream).

**Key insight:** RegGuard doesn't replace law firms; it makes them more efficient. Consultants become *distribution channel*.
